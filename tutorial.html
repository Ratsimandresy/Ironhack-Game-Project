Game Tutorial

Welcome to the Fruit Eater Game

Eat as many FRUITS as you can because the fruit-Eater is VERY hungry

Use KEYBOARD ARROW to control him

Each fruits gives you points

  • Strawberry : 3 points
  • Cherry : 2 points
  • orange : 2 points
  • Guava : 1 points

the EVIL BALL will try to take your life out

When the balls touch you, your points decrease

And avoid them or you will die!!!!
